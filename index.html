About SwapMate

SwapMate is a peer-to-peer platform where users can trade items they no longer need for things they want. It's easy to use, eco-friendly, and helps reduce waste while fostering a community of sharing.

How it Works

1. List Your Items

Upload the items you want to swap with photos and descriptions.

2. Browse Listings

Find items from other users that you're interested in.

3. Swap & Save

Trade items directly with others and save money while reducing waste.
